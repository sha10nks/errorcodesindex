<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(AE &mdash; Leak detected)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the appliance detected a potential water leak condition.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">AE appears when internal sensors register moisture in areas outside the normal wash chamber. The appliance activates protection to prevent damage. This detection does not automatically confirm a major leak. It reflects a safety monitoring trigger. The system pauses to prevent continued operation.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">During active cycle</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">After fill stage</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">On control display</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Moisture sensor activated</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Leak detection threshold exceeded</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Safety shutdown triggered</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Cycle stops</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Pump may activate</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Appliance remains paused</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a motor failure</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a heating issue</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a control board error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related error codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">E15 &mdash; Leak protection activated</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">LE &mdash; Leak detected (Samsung)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">E4 &mdash; Overflow condition</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Notes and edge cases</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Excess suds or condensation may occasionally trigger detection.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Optional next actions</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Power off appliance</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Allow area to dry</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Restart if cleared</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">External help (optional)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Repeated leak alerts should be evaluated by qualified service personnel.</span></p>
