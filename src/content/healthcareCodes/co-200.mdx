<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-200 &mdash; Non-covered service)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the billed service is not covered under the payer&rsquo;s policy.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-200 is applied when a service falls outside covered benefits. This determination is based on plan design rather than billing accuracy. It does not reflect service quality. Coverage exclusions vary by payer and plan.</span></p>
