<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(M303 &mdash; Administrative denial)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the claim was denied for administrative reasons.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">M303 is used when CMS denies a claim due to administrative requirements not being met. This is not a clinical or medical necessity decision. Administrative denials may relate to eligibility, documentation, or submission rules. The service itself may still be valid.</span></p>
