<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SYSTEM: Eligibility &amp; Coverage (270/271)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: AAA-75</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(AAA-75 &mdash; Subscriber not found)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the subscriber information provided could not be located in the payer&rsquo;s eligibility system.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">AAA-75 is returned when the subscriber record cannot be matched during an eligibility inquiry. The subscriber is typically the policyholder under whose coverage the patient is enrolled. This response occurs before eligibility determination and reflects a data identification issue. It does not indicate that coverage is inactive or terminated. The system cannot proceed without locating the subscriber record.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">271 eligibility response transactions</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Eligibility verification tools</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Clearinghouse eligibility reports</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Subscriber identifiers do not match payer records</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Incorrect policy or group information submitted</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Enrollment data not yet updated</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Eligibility details are not returned</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The inquiry remains unresolved</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">A new inquiry may be necessary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a coverage termination code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a claim denial</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a patient responsibility indicator</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related error codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">AAA-72 &mdash; Patient not found</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">AAA-82 &mdash; Invalid subscriber ID</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Notes and edge cases</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Subscriber records may change due to employment or plan updates. Timing differences between systems can temporarily affect lookup results.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Optional next actions</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Confirm subscriber identifiers</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Review policyholder information</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Verify enrollment effective dates</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">External help (optional)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Eligibility specialists may help reconcile subscriber record mismatches.</span></p>
