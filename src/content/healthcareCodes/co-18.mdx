<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SYSTEM: Claim Adjustment Reason Codes (CARC)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: CO-18</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-18 &mdash; Duplicate claim/service)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the claim or service has already been submitted and processed.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-18 is applied when a payer identifies a duplicate submission for the same service. The system detects matching claim details such as date, procedure, and provider. This prevents duplicate payment. It does not imply wrongdoing or error beyond duplication. The original claim typically determines final payment.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Claim status reports</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Duplicate claim notifications</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The same claim submitted multiple times</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Resubmission without required indicators</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">System resends due to transmission issues</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The duplicate claim is denied</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The original claim remains valid</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">No additional payment is issued</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a coverage denial</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a billing quality issue</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not fraud</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related error codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-98 &mdash; Charges already adjudicated</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-23 &mdash; Prior payer impact</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Notes and edge cases</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Some resubmissions are allowed with corrected information. Timing overlaps can create temporary duplicates.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Optional next actions</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Verify original claim status</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Confirm resubmission requirements</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Monitor duplicate prevention processes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">External help (optional)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Billing teams may assist in identifying duplicate submission patterns.</span></p>
