<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SYSTEM: Patient Responsibility (PR)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: PR-3</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(PR-3 &mdash; Copayment)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that a fixed copayment amount is the patient&rsquo;s responsibility.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">PR-3 reflects a fixed cost-sharing amount required under the patient&rsquo;s plan. Copayments are predetermined and apply to specific services. This is a standard plan feature and does not indicate denial. The remaining portion may be covered by insurance.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">EOB documents</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Patient billing statements</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Copayment applies to the service type</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Plan benefit rules enforced</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Service category requires copayment</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Patient is billed for the copayment</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Insurance covers remaining eligible charges</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Claim processing completes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a non-covered service</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a billing error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a deductible amount</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related error codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">PR-1 &mdash; Deductible amount</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">PR-2 &mdash; Coinsurance</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Notes and edge cases</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Copayment amounts vary by service and plan. Some preventive services may not require copayments.</span></p>
