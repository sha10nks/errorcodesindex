<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(M59 &mdash; Incomplete claim)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the Medicare claim was incomplete and could not be fully processed.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">M59 is applied when required claim components are missing or incomplete. CMS cannot adjudicate incomplete claims. This is an administrative issue rather than a coverage decision. Once the missing elements are addressed, the claim may be eligible for reprocessing.</span></p>
