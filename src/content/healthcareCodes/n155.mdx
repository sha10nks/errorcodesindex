<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(N155 &mdash; Service not reimbursable)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the service is not eligible for reimbursement under payer rules.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">N155 is used when a payer determines that a service is not reimbursable. This is a policy-based determination rather than a billing error. It does not imply that the service was inappropriate. Reimbursement eligibility depends on plan and payer guidelines.</span></p>
