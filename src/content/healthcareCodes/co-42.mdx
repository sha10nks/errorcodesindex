<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-42 &mdash; Charges exceed provider&rsquo;s contract)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the billed charges exceed the amount allowed under the provider&rsquo;s contract.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-42 is used when charges exceed contractual payment limits. The adjustment reflects agreed reimbursement rates. It is not a coverage denial or billing error. Contractual limits cap allowable charges.</span></p>
