<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-24 &mdash; Charges not covered)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the charges are not covered under the terms of the patient&rsquo;s insurance plan.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-24 is applied when billed charges fall outside the coverage parameters defined by the payer. This determination is based on plan design and benefit rules. It does not reflect a billing error or missing information. The service may be valid but excluded from reimbursement. Coverage exclusions vary by policy and payer.</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Claim adjudication summaries</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Charges are excluded under plan benefits</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Coverage limitations apply to the service</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Policy restrictions are enforced</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Payment for the charges is denied</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Responsibility may transfer to the patient</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The claim line is closed</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a documentation error</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a contractual adjustment</span></p>
