<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SYSTEM: Patient Responsibility (PR)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: PR-1</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(PR-1 &mdash; Deductible amount)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the charge was applied to the patient&rsquo;s deductible.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">PR-1 reflects the portion of the claim that the patient is responsible for under their deductible. Deductibles represent amounts patients must pay before insurance coverage begins. This is a standard cost-sharing mechanism, not a denial. The service itself may be covered, but payment is applied toward deductible limits.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Explanation of Benefits statements</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Patient billing summaries</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The deductible has not been met</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The service is subject to deductible rules</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Annual deductible resets apply</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The patient balance reflects the deductible amount</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The claim is finalized</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remaining coverage applies after deductible is met</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a non-covered service</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a billing error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a denial</span></p>
