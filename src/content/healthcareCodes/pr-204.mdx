<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(PR-204 &mdash; Service not covered under patient plan)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the service is not covered and is the patient&rsquo;s responsibility.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">PR-204 assigns financial responsibility to the patient for a service excluded from coverage. This reflects plan design rather than claim submission issues. The service may be valid but not reimbursable. This code clarifies responsibility rather than denying the claim entirely.</span></p>
