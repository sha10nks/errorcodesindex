<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SYSTEM: Claim Adjustment Reason Codes (CARC)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: CO-27</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-27 &mdash; Coverage terminated)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the patient&rsquo;s insurance coverage was not active on the date of service.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-27 is applied when a payer determines that coverage had ended before the service date. The claim is denied because the policy was no longer in force at the time services were rendered. This is a coverage status issue rather than a service-level determination. It does not indicate that the service was inappropriate or incorrectly billed. Coverage termination may occur due to policy expiration, cancellation, or eligibility changes.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Explanation of Benefits (EOB)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Eligibility and claim status portals</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Policy coverage ended prior to service date</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Eligibility records were not updated in time</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Coverage lapsed due to non-renewal</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The claim is denied by the payer</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Responsibility may shift to another insurer or the patient</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The claim is closed unless redirected</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a non-covered service determination</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a billing format error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a temporary processing delay</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related error codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-109 &mdash; Claim not covered by this payer</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-22 &mdash; Other payer may be responsible</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Notes and edge cases</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Coverage may terminate retroactively in some situations. Coordination of benefits issues can temporarily trigger this code until corrected.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Optional next actions</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Review eligibility confirmation records</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Check for alternate or secondary coverage</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">External help (optional)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Insurance coordinators may help clarify coverage termination timelines.</span></p>
