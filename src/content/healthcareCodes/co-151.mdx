<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SYSTEM: Claim Adjustment Reason Codes (CARC)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: CO-151</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-151 &mdash; Payment adjusted due to contractual agreement)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the payment amount was adjusted according to the provider&rsquo;s contract with the payer.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-151 represents a contractual adjustment applied under negotiated reimbursement terms. The payer reduces the billed amount to match agreed-upon contract rates. This is a standard adjustment and does not indicate an error or denial. It reflects contractual obligations rather than coverage limitations. The adjusted amount is typically not billable to the patient.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Provider remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Contractual adjustment reports</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">EOB statements</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Contracted rates differ from billed charges</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Network participation agreements apply</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Standard reimbursement policies enforced</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Payment is finalized at the adjusted amount</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The adjusted balance is written off</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">No additional claim action occurs</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a denial</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not patient responsibility</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a billing error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related error codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-45 &mdash; Charge exceeds fee schedule</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-42 &mdash; Contractual allowance exceeded</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Notes and edge cases</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Different service lines may have different contract rates. Out-of-network claims may not use this adjustment. Multiple contractual adjustments may appear on one claim.</span></p>
