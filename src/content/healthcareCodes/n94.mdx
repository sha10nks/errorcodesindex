<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(N94 &mdash; Processed in excess of charges)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the payment calculation exceeded the billed charges.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">N94 is used when claim processing results in amounts that exceed the billed charges. The payer adjusts the payment to align with actual charges. This remark clarifies why an adjustment occurred. It does not indicate an error by the provider or patient. The final payment reflects correct charge limits.</span></p>
