<p class="c1"><span class="c0">SECTION: Healthcare Insurance / Medical Billing</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: CO-204</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-204 &mdash; Service not covered under plan)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the service is excluded from coverage under the patient&rsquo;s insurance plan.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-204 is applied when a payer determines that a specific service is not included in the plan&rsquo;s covered benefits. The denial is based on plan design rather than claim submission accuracy. It reflects an exclusion or limitation within the policy. This does not imply that the service was unnecessary or incorrectly provided. Coverage exclusions vary by plan type and policy terms.</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Remittance advice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Claim adjudication summaries</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The service is excluded under plan benefits</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Coverage limits apply to the service category</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Plan design restrictions are enforced</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Payment for the service is denied</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Responsibility may transfer to the patient</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The claim line is closed</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a billing error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a processing delay</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a contractual adjustment</span></p>
