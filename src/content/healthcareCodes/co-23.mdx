<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-23 &mdash; Impact of prior payer adjudication)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that claim processing was affected by a prior payer&rsquo;s adjudication.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-23 is used when a payer considers the outcome of another payer&rsquo;s adjudication. This reflects coordination of benefits processing. The code does not independently determine coverage. It clarifies that payment decisions account for prior payments or denials. Final reimbursement depends on payer order and rules.</span></p>
