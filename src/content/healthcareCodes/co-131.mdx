<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CO-131 &mdash; Claim-specific negotiated discount)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that a negotiated discount was applied to the claim.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CO-131 is used when a payer applies a specific negotiated discount to a claim. This reflects contractual arrangements between the payer and provider. It is not a denial or billing error. The discount adjusts payment to agreed terms. Patient responsibility is typically not affected.</span></p>
