<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that a content license could not be validated by PlayStation Network.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">NP-31805-7 appears when PSN cannot confirm a license associated with digital content. Licensing controls access to purchased or downloaded items. This issue occurs at the account or service validation layer. It does not indicate a hardware fault. License checks may fail temporarily.</span></p>
