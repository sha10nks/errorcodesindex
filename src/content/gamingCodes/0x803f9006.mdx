<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates a problem validating a license for digital content.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">0x803F9006 appears when Xbox cannot verify a license entitlement. The issue occurs at the account or service validation layer. It does not imply a completed purchase failure. The code reflects a temporary or persistent entitlement issue. License checks may fail during service disruptions.</span></p>
