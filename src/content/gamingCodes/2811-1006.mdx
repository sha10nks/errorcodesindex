<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(2811-1006 &mdash; Download error)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that a download could not be completed successfully.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">2811-1006 appears when a download process fails during retrieval or installation. The issue relates to data transfer or validation rather than licensing. It does not indicate account enforcement. The download may stop partway through. Service or connectivity conditions may be involved.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Game or update downloads</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Download progress interruptions</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Error notifications during downloads</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Download interruption</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Data transfer inconsistency</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Temporary service instability</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Download stops</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Content remains incomplete</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Retry may be required later</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a payment decline</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a license error</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a console ban</span></p>
