<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that the console was unable to sign in to Xbox Live due to a network-related issue.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">0x87DD0005 appears when Xbox Live sign-in cannot be completed because communication with the service fails. The issue occurs at the connectivity or service-access layer. It does not indicate an account enforcement action. The error reflects an inability to establish or maintain a session with Xbox Live services. Local network conditions or service availability may be involved.</span></p>
