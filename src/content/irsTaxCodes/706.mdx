<p class="c1"><span class="c0">CODE: 706</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(706 &mdash; Interest adjustment)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that an adjustment to previously assessed interest has been applied to the account.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Code 706 reflects a modification to the interest previously calculated on the account. The adjustment may increase or decrease the total interest owed depending on the circumstances. This entry represents a recalculation based on updated balances, payments, or corrections. The code does not specify the reason for the change beyond indicating interest modification. It reflects administrative recalculation within IRS systems.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">On IRS account transcripts</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">After payments or balance changes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">In connection with other adjustment entries</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">A payment altered interest calculations</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Account balance changed due to assessment or reduction</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Administrative correction required recalculation</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Timing of payment affected interest accrual</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">The account balance may update</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Additional interest entries may appear</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Payment or offset activity may follow</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a tax assessment</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a refund issuance</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It does not represent principal tax amount</span></p>
