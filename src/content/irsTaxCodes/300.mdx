<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(300 &mdash; Additional tax assessed)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that additional tax was assessed, often related to examination or review findings.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Code 300 reflects an additional tax assessment posted to the account, typically associated with examination results or formal adjustments. It indicates that liability increased following review. The code represents the official posting of additional assessed tax. It does not provide detail about the underlying reason beyond its association with review outcomes. The entry typically includes the assessed amount.</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Compliance action resulted in assessment</span></p>

<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Account balance increases</span></p>
