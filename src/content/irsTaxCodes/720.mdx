<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that interest has accrued on the outstanding tax balance.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Code 720 reflects the addition of accrued interest to the account. This entry represents interest calculated on unpaid balances over time. It increases the total amount owed until payments or adjustments reduce the principal. The code does not indicate penalties or new tax assessments, only interest accumulation. It reflects ongoing financial calculation within IRS systems.</span></p>
