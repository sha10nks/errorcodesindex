<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(Refund rejected &mdash; Refund not approved for issuance)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This status indicates that the refund was not approved in its original form and processing did not result in issuance.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">&ldquo;Refund rejected&rdquo; reflects that refund issuance was not completed as originally calculated. It may result from adjustments, review findings, or discrepancies identified during processing. The status does not necessarily indicate wrongdoing. Transcript entries typically show assessment or adjustment codes explaining changes. It represents denial or modification of expected refund amount.</span></p>
