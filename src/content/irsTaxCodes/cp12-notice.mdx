<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CP12 notice &mdash; Math error correction notice)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This notice indicates that the IRS corrected a mathematical or clerical error on the return.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">A CP12 notice reflects that the IRS made an adjustment due to a mathematical or calculation error identified during processing. It indicates that the original figures were modified to correct discrepancies. The notice explains the adjustment and its impact on refund or balance. It does not represent an audit or fraud determination. The transcript may show related adjustment codes reflecting the correction.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">In mailed IRS correspondence</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">On account transcripts with adjustment codes</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">During refund recalculation</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Calculation error on return</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Mismatch in reported amounts</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Clerical correction required</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">System-detected arithmetic discrepancy</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Refund amount may change</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Account balance may adjust</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Adjustment codes may post</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a freeze</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not an audit notice</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It does not automatically indicate penalty</span></p>
