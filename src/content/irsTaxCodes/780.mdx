<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(780 &mdash; Credit transferred)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This code indicates that a credit amount has been transferred to or from another tax account or period.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Code 780 reflects movement of a credit between accounts or tax periods. This entry shows that funds or credits were reallocated administratively. It does not indicate refund issuance or payment receipt by itself. The code represents internal account management to ensure balances are applied appropriately. The transcript typically shows the amount transferred and related posting date.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Where users usually see this code</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">On IRS account transcripts</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">After adjustments involving multiple tax years</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">During review of balance reconciliation</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Why this code usually appears</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">A credit was applied to another tax period</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Overpayment was reallocated</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Administrative correction required transfer</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Offset procedures moved funds between accounts</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What typically happens next</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Account balances adjust accordingly</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Related entries may appear on another tax year transcript</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Additional adjustment or notice codes may post</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code is NOT</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a refund issuance</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It is not a payment receipt</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">It does not represent new tax liability</span></p>
