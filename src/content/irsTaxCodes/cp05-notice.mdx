<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(CP05 notice &mdash; Refund under review notice)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This notice indicates that the IRS is reviewing the return and additional time is required before issuing the refund.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">A CP05 notice reflects that the IRS has selected the return for review before releasing the refund. It signals that processing is temporarily paused while verification procedures are conducted. The notice does not indicate an audit by default but represents review activity. It does not confirm denial or adjustment at the time issued. The notice explains that additional time is needed.</span></p>
