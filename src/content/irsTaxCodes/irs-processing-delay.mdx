<p class="c1"><span class="c0">Title</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">(IRS processing delay &mdash; Extended processing time)</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">One-sentence summary</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">This status indicates that the tax return or refund is taking longer than standard processing timelines.</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">What this code means</span></p>
<p class="c1"><span class="c0">&ldquo;IRS processing delay&rdquo; reflects extended processing beyond typical timeframes. It does not automatically indicate audit, freeze, or denial. The delay may result from review procedures, verification requirements, or administrative workload. Transcript entries may provide more detailed explanation than summary messages. The status represents timing rather than a specific transaction code.</span></p>
